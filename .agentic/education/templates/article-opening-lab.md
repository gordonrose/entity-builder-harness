## Comparison

Compare the candidates.

Answer:

- Which opening makes the reader feel the cost fastest?
- Which opening is most retellable by a cold reader?
- Which opening relies least on abstract system language?
- Which opening best bridges from the reader's world into the source story?
- Which opening is grounded in approved source material rather than an
  invented bridge?
- Which opening gives the reader an honest route to the named artifacts, or
  plainly explains why that route is missing?
- Which opening gives the writer enough runway to add reader-world examples
  before the thesis?
- Which opening best teaches the prerequisite ladder before the article uses
  technical vocabulary or a harness mechanism?
- Which opening should be used, revised, or rejected?
