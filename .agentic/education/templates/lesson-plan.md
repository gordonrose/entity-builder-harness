## Audits

### Specificity Audit

- Which concrete evidence supports the lesson?
- Could this have been written without reading the source logs?
- Is there a real incident, tradeoff, mistake, or surprise?

### Audience Audit

- Does the lesson make learners feel upgraded rather than exposed?
- Is the surface tone positive and opportunity-facing?
- Is critique present as subtext, contrast, implication, or upgrade path?

### Teaching Audit

- What will learners understand afterward?
- Does the 30-minute core stand alone?
- Does the 50-minute extension deepen rather than distract?
- Is there one main idea at a time?
- Is there a worked example before abstraction?
- Is there a retrieval, prediction, or practice moment?

### AI-Smell Audit

- Are positive claims anchored in concrete evidence?
- Are significance inflation, vague authority, and promotional phrasing removed?
- Does specificity earn the lift?
