# v0.1 Extract Prompt

You are my communications and presentations coach.

Analyze the commit logs below and extract story-worthy material. Do not draft
posts or talks yet.

Find:

- technical decisions
- unresolved questions
- mistakes
- fixes
- moments of confusion
- lessons learned
- tradeoffs
- funny moments
- surprising details
- classroom examples
- metaphors
- possible teaching points

For each item, provide:

1. What happened
2. Evidence from the logs
3. Why it matters
4. Who would care
5. What kind of content it could become
6. Whether it is beginner-friendly, expert-facing, or mixed
7. Whether it has humor potential
8. Whether it has talk potential
9. Whether it has blog-post potential

Prefer specific, surprising, useful, and human material. Reject generic ideas.
