# v0.1 Candidates Prompt

Using the extracted material below, generate candidate content ideas.

Create:

- 10 candidate 3-minute blog posts
- 5 candidate 30-minute talks
- 5 candidate 30-minute lesson plans with 50-minute extension paths
- 10 teaching assets
- 5 possible recurring series ideas

For each idea, explain:

- premise
- hook
- lesson
- story arc
- audience
- comedic angle
- risk
- evidence from the logs
- why this is worth making

For lesson-plan candidates, also include:

- 30-minute learning promise
- 50-minute extension
- worked example
- participant activity
- debrief question

Do not write full drafts yet.

Prioritize ideas that are specific, surprising, useful, and human. Remove any
idea that could have been generated without reading the logs.
