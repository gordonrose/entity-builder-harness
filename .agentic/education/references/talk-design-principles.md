# Talk Design Principles

Use these as durable quality principles, not as source imitation.

- A 30-minute talk needs one central spine.
- Slides should support the speaker, not become an essay.
- Include audience interaction through prediction, reflection, voting, or
  debugging.
- Use timing guidance to avoid overstuffing.
- Plan what not to explain.
- Close by returning to the central story or practical promise.
