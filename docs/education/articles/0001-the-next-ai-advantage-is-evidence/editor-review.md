The article now passes the reader-world research gate and the line-30

- The opening is fresh, source-backed, and names the actual reported story
  rather than hiding behind a generic "major consulting firm."

  before being asked to accept the thesis.

- The harness story now works as a smaller, author-owned version of the public
  pattern.
- The line-30 gate is much stronger: world, object, AI temptation, downside,
  and personal relevance are all visible.

  not become a list.
